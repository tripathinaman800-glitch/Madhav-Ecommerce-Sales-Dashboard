# Business Insights

## Sales Performance

- Maharashtra generated the highest sales revenue.
- A significant portion of sales was concentrated among a few customers.

## Product Analysis

- Clothing accounted for the largest share of total quantity sold.
- Electronics and Furniture contributed smaller portions of sales volume.

## Profit Analysis

- Printers generated the highest profit among all sub-categories.
- Bookcases were another major contributor to profitability.

## Payment Analysis

- Cash on Delivery (COD) was the most preferred payment method.
- UPI emerged as the second most used payment option.

## Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-performing states.
- Promote profitable sub-categories through targeted campaigns.
- Encourage digital payment adoption through offers and incentives.
